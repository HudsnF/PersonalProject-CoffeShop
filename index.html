Coffee is a beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans. Darkly colored, bitter, and slightly acidic, coffee has a stimulating effect on humans, primarily due to its caffeine content. It has the highest sales in the world market for hot drinks.

The physical characteristics and chemical composition of coffee are influenced by by factors of different natures, highlighting among them genetic, environmental, nutritional factors, management of plowing, harvesting, preparation, etc. With the exception of genetic and environmental, the other factors can be controlled after implementation of the crop, not only in management (good agronomic practices), when every effort is put into obtaining of the maximum in quality, as well as in the phase harvesting and coffee preparation, in which seeks to preserve the quality obtained.
